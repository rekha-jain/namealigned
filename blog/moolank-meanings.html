Your Moolank is the single most permanent number in your entire Chaldean chart. It cannot be changed, adjusted or corrected — it is the pure vibrational signature of the day you were born. What is Moolank?

The word Moolank comes from Sanskrit — mool meaning root, and ank meaning number. It is the root number, the original vibration, the number that defines the basic pattern of who you are at your core. In Western Chaldean numerology, the same concept is called the Driver Number or Psychic Number.

The Moolank is calculated from the day of birth alone — not the month, not the year, just the date. If you were born on the 5th of any month, your Moolank is 5. If you were born on the 23rd, your Moolank is 2+3 = 5. Three different birth dates — the 5th, the 14th, and the 23rd — all yield the same Moolank of 5, which means they share the same planetary ruler (Mercury) and the same fundamental energy signature.

Moolank is the number of your immediate personality — how you instinctively behave, how you present yourself to the world, what kind of first impression you make. It governs your outer character, your material life, the way you approach decisions, and the type of energy you radiate in a room.

Important: In Chaldean numerology, Moolank is calculated from the day of birth only — not the full date of birth. That full-date calculation gives you the Bhagyank (destiny number). The two numbers are distinct and measure different dimensions of your chart.

How to calculate your Moolank

The calculation is simple. Take the day you were born. If it is a single digit (1–9), that is your Moolank directly. If it is a two-digit number (10–31), add the two digits together and reduce to a single digit.

Quick example: Born on the 14th → 1 + 4 = 5. Your Moolank is 5, your ruling planet is Mercury.

Why Moolank matters more than your sun sign

Your Moolank has three qualities that make it uniquely powerful as a self-knowledge tool.

It is permanent. Unlike your name number, which can be corrected, or your circumstances, which change — your Moolank is fixed the moment you are born. It is the one number in your chart that cannot be altered, adjusted or improved. This makes it the purest signal in numerology.

It is planetary. Each Moolank is directly ruled by one of the nine celestial bodies in Vedic-Chaldean cosmology. This connects your birth date to real, observable astronomical cycles — not merely a symbolic framework. Your planet determines your lucky day, your power colours, your compatible gemstone and your natural timing cycles.

It is measurable. Unlike sun signs, which divide the year into twelve vague 30-day windows, Moolank has nine distinct types and each day of the month maps precisely to one of them. The system is granular, calculable, and directly connected to the compound number energy of your specific birth date.

Cheiro wrote in his Book of Numbers: "The birth number governs the outer personality, the physical body and the material conditions of life. No matter how great the outer fortune or circumstance, the birth number remains the key to the individual's real self."

Cheiro noted of Sun-ruled individuals: "They are born to lead, and when they resist this calling, they feel a persistent inner restlessness that no outer success can fully resolve."
