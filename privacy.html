Privacy Policy

Plain language · IT Act 2000 compliant · Last updated April 2026

📅 Last updated: April 2026 🔒 IT Act 2000 compliant 📧 Questions: privacy@namealigned.com
The short version: We collect only what you give us to run your numerology analysis. We never sell your data. We store it securely. You can ask us to delete it anytime by emailing privacy@namealigned.com.

1. Who We Are

NameAligned.com ("we", "us", "our") is a numerology analysis platform operated from India. Our website is located at https://namealigned.com. You can reach us at privacy@namealigned.com.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services. By using NameAligned.com, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect only information you voluntarily provide to us. We do not collect any information without your knowledge.

Information you give us directly

  • Name: Your full name, as you enter it for numerology analysis. This is the primary input to our tool.
  • Date of Birth: Required to calculate your Moolank (birth number) and Bhagyank (destiny number).
  • Email address: Collected at the time of payment for the ₹199 detailed report, to deliver your report to you. Also collected if you sign up for our newsletter.
  • Mobile number: Optional. Collected only if you use the mobile number analysis feature. Not required for the main analysis.

Information collected automatically

  • Analytics data: We use basic analytics (page views, session duration, device type) to understand how our site is used and to improve it. This data is aggregated and not tied to any individual.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and referring URL — standard server log data collected automatically by our hosting infrastructure.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide only for the purposes you would reasonably expect:

  • To generate your numerology analysis: Your name and date of birth are used to compute your numerological numbers and produce your analysis results. This is the core function of our service.
  • To deliver your paid report: If you purchase the ₹199 detailed report, we use your email address to send you the report. This is the primary reason we collect email at the payment stage.
  • To send occasional product updates: If you have provided your email, we may send you occasional updates about new features or insights. Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link. We do not send more than two non-report emails per month.
  • To process payments: Payment is handled by Razorpay. We do not see or store your card details — only the payment confirmation and your email/name for report delivery.
  • To improve the service: Aggregated, anonymised usage data helps us understand which features are most useful and where to invest development effort.

4. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored on Supabase, a secure database platform. Our Supabase instance is configured to use the India (ap-south-1) region, meaning your data is stored on servers located in India.

  • Data is encrypted at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption.
  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS at all times — we do not allow unencrypted connections.
  • Access to the database is restricted to authorised personnel only, protected by strong authentication.
  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide you the service, or as required by Indian law.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Full stop.

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Razorpay (payment processor): When you make a payment, your name and email are shared with Razorpay to process the transaction. Razorpay is a licensed payment aggregator regulated by the Reserve Bank of India. Their privacy policy is available at razorpay.com/privacy.
  • Legal requirements: If required by Indian law, court order, or a lawful request by a government authority, we may disclose your information. We will notify you of such requests to the extent permitted by law.
  • Business transfer: In the event NameAligned.com is acquired or merged with another entity, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified by email before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies minimally and only for legitimate operational purposes.

  • Analytics cookies: We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate site usage. These do not track you across other websites and do not contain personally identifiable information.
  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies used to maintain your session while using the tool. These are deleted when you close your browser.
  • No advertising or tracking cookies: We do not use Facebook Pixel, Google Ads tracking, or any third-party advertising trackers. We have no interest in profiling you for advertising.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the analysis tool.

7. Your Rights

As a user of NameAligned.com, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to deletion: You can ask us to delete all personal data we hold about you. To exercise this right, email privacy@namealigned.com with the subject "Delete my data". We will process your request within 30 days.
  • Right to unsubscribe: Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can also email us at any time to opt out of all non-essential communications.
